John Herbert Company...The Flooring People


Our Company

John Herbert Company, first founded by Herbert Sandler as an office furniture dealer in 1954, is now a second generation commercial flooring company that specializes in all types of flooring and concrete resurfacing, servicing the Tri-State area.  The company’s arsenal of ride-on floor demo equipment,  concrete grinders with hepa vac systems, extensive labor pool, large inventory, and highly trained staff, allows it to take on the most challenging of projects and complete on time and on budget. From historical restorations to active surgical suite renovations, no project is too large or too small.  The company routinely performs work in the Hudson Valley’s most advanced medical centers, private universities, and development projects. John Herbert Company is a bonded NYS contractor with an aggregate bonding capacity surpassing $10 million, single project $3 million. All field personnel are 10 hour OSHA certified.


Services


Existing Floor Removal

John Herbert Company’s labor force has the ability to restore floors to a wood or concrete foundation and haul away existing products using specialized equipment including: “ride-on” floor strippers, walk-behind floor strippers, and industry specific hand tool.


Concrete Grinding & Toppings

Depending on our client’s unique needs, we offer a variety of options to to smooth, level, or strip away the top surface of existing floor foundations. Our fleet of concrete finishing equipment includes walk-behind shot-blasters, cement pumps, and grinders ranging from 3-inches up to 20-inch diamond infused walk-behind blades. Clients also have the option to integrate moisture barriers between layers of floor covering as well as protective and finishing coats for exposed concrete floors.


Hard and Soft Floor Covering

John Herbert Company’s salesforce possesses deep industry knowledge of specialty products to ensure that our clients must never compromise on function or form. We pride ourselves on our close relationship with industry manufacturers that allow us to deliver exceptional value and quality to our clients.
